This is a platform where we fellowship together in word and in prayer in order to unlock the things hid for us in Christ Jesus and to know the essence of our calling. it is an ordained platform to irrigate the body of Christ. it is a fountain overflowing with water of life to everyone that comes in contact of it.
Sagab Apostolic Network hangs on the promise of God for man: to set man on high (psalm 91:14), to comfort man (Matt.5:4), etc Christ is our life, our message, our energy, our strength, our wisdom, our ideas and our head where everything flows.
Sagab came from the Hebrew word which means to be safetly, highly and securely set up on high.

PROVERB 18:10
the name of the Lord is a strong tower: the righteous runneth into it and is safe.
PSALM 91:14
because he hath set in love upon me, therefore will i deliver Him: i will set him on high, because he hath know my name.
The word "i will set him on high" in psalm 91:14 explained the meaning "safe" in proverb 18:10. Because the same word "Sagab" is used for the both passages.
