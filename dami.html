SAGAB APOSTOLIC NETWORK...



OUR MISSION

By setting our love upon Christ through the fellowship of the spirit in His word and in prayers.

OUR VISSION

To gain ascendancy in the spirit where the laws of spirit of life in Christ Jesus find expression in us.

WHO WE ARE

Sagab network hangs on the promise of God for man: to set man on high (psalm 91:14), to comfort man (matt 5:4).

SAGAB MEANS

The word Sagab came from the Hebrew word which means to be safely, highly and securely set up on high.

FOUNDER

Ehinju Cornelius Simon from Akungba Aloko, Akoko South West Local Government of Ondo State, Nigeria. From the family of Ehinju (the late Alakunmi of Akunmi community, Akungba). I was raised in Kogi State, Kabba to be precise. This is where i had my primary and secondary school. I served under Nigria Christian Corpers Fellowship (NCCF) as a Zonal Coordinate in Onelga LGA of River State.
I gave my life to christ at tender age in Kabba and since then i began to have more interest in things concerning church with the mind to know God better. I was born and raise up in Christ Apostolic Church. I hold a National Diploma in Accounting, Bachelor Degree in Social Science Education and Bachelor Degree in Theology.
My philosophy is that there is a place in God that can attain and be secure from the enemy. The Bible say 'I will set you on high(SAGAB)'. This is the height that is beyond the reach of any other power and forces. This becomes ny pursuit and i really want to attain this height while on Earth here and also bring everyone around me to the same height in Christ Jesus.
